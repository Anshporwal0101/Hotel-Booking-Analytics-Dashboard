# 🏨 Hotel Booking Analytics Dashboard

## 📖 Overview

The Hotel Booking Analytics Dashboard is a Business Intelligence project developed using Microsoft Excel to analyze hotel booking data and transform raw records into meaningful insights.

The dashboard helps understand booking patterns, customer behavior, cancellation trends, revenue performance, and market segmentation. It provides decision-makers with an interactive tool to monitor hotel performance and identify opportunities for business growth.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Analyze hotel booking behavior.
- Identify factors affecting cancellations.
- Understand customer demographics and booking channels.
- Monitor revenue performance through ADR analysis.
- Discover seasonal booking trends.
- Support data-driven decision making in the hospitality industry.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains hotel booking information collected from City Hotels and Resort Hotels.

### Key Attributes

| Column | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Hotel | Hotel Type (City Hotel / Resort Hotel) |
| Is_Canceled | Booking cancellation status |
| Booking_Date | Date of booking |
| Arrival_Date | Customer arrival date |
| Lead_Time | Number of days between booking and arrival |
| Lead_Category | Short / Medium / Long lead time |
| Total_Stay_Nights | Total nights stayed |
| Stay_Category | Categorized stay duration |
| Country | Customer country |
| Market_Segment | Booking market segment |
| Distribution_Channel | Booking source |
| Is_Repeated_Guest | Repeat customer indicator |
| Deposit_Type | Booking deposit status |
| Customer_Type | Contract, Group, Transient, etc. |
| ADR | Average Daily Rate |

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 📌 High Cancellation Rate

Almost 48% of bookings are canceled, indicating a significant opportunity to improve booking confirmation and retention strategies.

---

### 📌 Online Travel Agencies Drive Most Bookings

Online Travel Agencies (OTA) represent the largest booking source, making them a crucial channel for customer acquisition.

---

### 📌 Portugal Generates the Highest Visitor Count

Most customers originate from Portugal, followed by other European countries.

---

### 📌 Revenue is Strongest Through Direct and Online Channels

Direct bookings and Online TA channels contribute higher ADR values compared to other segments.

---

### 📌 Repeat Customer Rate is Low

Only 2.05% of customers are repeat guests, highlighting the need for customer loyalty programs.

---

### 📌 Transient Customers Dominate

The majority of bookings come from transient customers, making them the primary customer segment.

---

### 📌 City Hotels Perform Better

City Hotels show stronger booking performance compared to Resort Hotels.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### 1. Reduce Cancellation Rate
- Implement flexible cancellation policies.
- Offer incentives for confirmed bookings.
- Introduce automated booking reminders.

### 2. Improve Customer Retention
- Launch loyalty and rewards programs.
- Provide personalized offers to returning guests.

### 3. Focus on High-Revenue Segments
- Increase marketing efforts toward Direct and Online TA customers.
- Promote premium room packages.

### 4. Expand High-Performing Markets
- Strengthen campaigns in Portugal and other top-performing countries.
- Develop region-specific promotions.

### 5. Enhance Demand Forecasting
- Use historical booking patterns to predict future occupancy levels.
